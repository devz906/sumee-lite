## Description

**Rediscover the classics with SUMEE.**

SUMEE is the elegant, privacy-focused way to organize and play your retro game collection on iPhone. Designed with a clean, modern interface that respects the nostalgia of the past, SUMEE brings your favorite consoles together in one beautiful app.

**WIDEST COMPATIBILITY**
Seamlessly import and play your backups for your favorite classic systems. From handheld heroes (DS, GBA) to home console legends (NES, SNES, PSX, Genesis), SUMEE handles them all with precision performance.

**YOUR CONSOLE, YOUR STYLE**
Make SUMEE truly yours.
- **Dynamic Themes:** Choose from stunning themes like "Christmas", "Bubbles", or the classic "Homebrew" aesthetic.
- **Custom Avatars:** Set up your profile with color-coordinated avatars or your own photos.
- **Controller Skins:** Beautiful, responsive touch controls designed for every system.

**PRIVACY FIRST & OFFLINE-READY**
SUMEE is built for you, not for tracking.
- **100% Offline:** No login required, no tracking, and no data collection. Your library stays on your device.
- **Local Storage:** Your saves, states, and games are yours alone.

**PRO FEATURES**
- **Controller Support:** Connect your favorite Bluetooth controller for the authentic console feel.
- **Files Integration:** Easily import your legal backups directly from the Files app.
- **Smooth Emulation:** Optimized for the latest iPhones with high-performance rendering.

*Note: SUMEE is an emulation utility. No games or copyrighted system files are included. Users must provide their own legally owned game files.*
